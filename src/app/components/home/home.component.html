Welcome to BetterWish!

The better way to keep track of your wishes.

How to manage your wishlists

  • With BetterWish, you can store wishes in wishlists for different topics.
  • To create a new wishlist, click on "Add new wishlist" on the left.
  • To delete a wishlist, click on the trash icon.
  • To edit a wishlist, click on the edit icon.

    • How to manage your wishes

  • Within a wishlist, click on the yellow plus sign on the bottom right to add a new wish to the list.
  • Next to the name, the description and the price, you can also add a url, for example to a web page where the product is available. You can also indicate an image url. Once you are done, please click on "Save". The wish appears in your list.
  • You can click on the link icon or the image to open up the url you stored for your wish.
  • If you have already bought the desired item, you can mark it as purchased.
  • If you want to delete a wish, please click on the delete icon.
  • To edit a wish, please click on the edit icon.
