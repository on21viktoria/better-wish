Welcome to Better wish!

The better way to keep track of your wishes.

How to manage your wishlists

  • With Better wish, you can store all your wishes in wishlists for different topics.
  • To create a new wishlist, click on "Add new wishlist" on the left.
  • To delete a wishlist, click on the delete icon. To update it, click on the update icon.

    • How to manage your wishes

  • Within your wishlists, click on the yellow plus sign to add new wishes to the list.
  • You can click on the link icon to open up the url you stored for this specific link.
  • If you have already bought the desired item, you can mark it as purchased.
  • If you want to delete an icon, please click on the delete icon.
